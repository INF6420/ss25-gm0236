Using the Antioch Rebroadcaster, you can play old-time radio content to your Part 15 AM or FM transmitter and play your own station IDs for your home and your neighbors.

This project is legacy supported and only updated for new stream addresses. It will not work on Catalina and higher on the Mac. It runs up to Windows 11, but in my experience there are sound drops a couple times a minute perhaps due to the Quicktime version

Download the Antioch Rebroadcaster for Mac (Mac OS X 10.4 Tiger - macOS 10.14 Mojave (PowerPC and Intel))

Download the Antioch Rebroadcaster for Windows (requires Quicktime, Windows XP or higher)
